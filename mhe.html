Reading Leadership Symposium

Macmillan/McGraw-Hill

Further opportunities to set the bar higher for the building brighter futures campaign; a symposium was initiated to educate sales reps about the Reading Leadership program. This symposium look and feel was designed around New Mexico’s culture and featured author speakers and a chance to explore the city amongst other activities.

Video Mailer

Along with brochures and print materials, drop offs and agendas, a video was created as a mailer invitation describing the activities that one could learn enjoy at the symposium. The future of sales and education have never looked brighter.
